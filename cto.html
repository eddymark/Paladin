CTO at Social Justice Startup

New York,NY


The CTO is a founding team role that sets the vision for the company’s tech platform and leads the creation of intuitive, impactful software that helps professionals change lives.


Paladin is streamlining corporate social responsibility (CSR) by building a global pro bono marketplace where lawyers can connect with personalized pro bono opportunities and companies can track their positive impact.

We recently closed pre-seed funding by top investors and were accepted into a premier New York City-based accelerator. Its founding team has a track record of both legal pro bono expertise and scaling a YCombinator company across the country.

    Key Responsibilitie Include:

  • Own all technology strategy and decision making including tech stack and architecture
  • Build out the architecture and tech stack to enable Paladin to realize our mission
  • Grow and lead the engineering team
  • Be the voice of technology within the Paladin team
  • Develop and deliver the product roadmap to update Paladin as the market evolves
  • Manage day-to-day of engineering processes

    Qualifications:

  • Proficiency in modern web and mobile technologies
  • 5+ years of experience, preferably in a start-up environment
  • Comfortable building out and scaling technology in a small, effective and iterative environment
  • Enjoys and has experience working in (and leading, preferably) small teams
  • Excellent communication skills with colleagues

    What We Offer:

  • Meaningful and impactful mission of increasing access to justice for those who need it most
  • Opportunity for rapid growth as we continue to grow
  • Benefits package and equity ownership potential /li>
  • Fast-paced, exciting and fun startup environment within a premier accelerator program
  • Dedicated, passionate and experienced team (with backgrounds at Skadden, the United Nations, the Justice Department and Expa) committed to driving Paladin’s success
