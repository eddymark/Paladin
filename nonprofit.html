Head of Nonprofit Relations at Social Justice Startup

New York,NY


The Head of Nonprofit Relations is a founding team role that shapes Paladin’s relationship with partner nonprofits, helping them to increase their pool of talented pro bono attorneys and build relationships with new companies, law firms, and law schools.


Paladin is streamlining corporate social responsibility (CSR) by building a global pro bono marketplace where lawyers can connect with personalized pro bono opportunities and companies can track their positive impact.

We recently closed pre-seed funding by top investors and were accepted into a premier New York City-based accelerator. Its founding team has a track record of both legal pro bono expertise and scaling a YCombinator company across the country.

    Key Responsibilitie Include:

  • Research potential nonprofit partners and develop an outreach strategy
  • Organize and execute outreach efforts, including presentations
  • Manage nonprofit partner onboarding and pro bono case identification
  • Match volunteer attorneys with nonprofit partners and provide case support
  • Build relationships with existing partners, troubleshoot when necessary, and incorporate feedback into Paladin’s processes to ensure the greatest value-add

    Qualifications:

  • Strong organizational skills and reliability
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Track record of managing partnerships and/or client relations
  • Collaborative and outgoing personality
  • 4+ years experience in nonprofit relations or pro bono preferred

    What We Offer:

  • Meaningful and impactful mission of increasing access to justice for those who need it most
  • Opportunity for rapid growth as we continue to grow
  • Benefits package and equity ownership potential /li>
  • Fast-paced, exciting and fun startup environment within a premier accelerator program
  • Dedicated, passionate and experienced team (with backgrounds at Skadden, the United Nations, the Justice Department and Expa) committed to driving Paladin’s success
